adidas wants to hear from you!
At adidas, we want our customers to make their first choice the right choice - and your product feedback helps them do exactly that. Anyone can submit reviews, regardless of whether they bought their product online on in a retail store, we just ask you to follow the guidelines and rules below.
How to write a great review
  • Focus on the product and your personal experience using it
  • Provide details on why you liked or disliked the product, not just whether you liked it or not
  • Try to give the kind of information that you would have liked to have known before purchasing. How does it fit, how comfortable is it etc. Any information that would help you in making a decision to purchase something can be very valuable to others.
  • Be honest and sincere. Provided you follow the rules (see What to avoid) we publish every review we receive, positive or negative. Your feedback not only helps other customers make informed buying decisions, but help us develop even better products.
  • Keep it to the point and useful. The minimum number of characters is 50. Make it too long and people will lose interest. If you are going to the effort of writing a review, you want to it be read, don’t you?
What to avoid
As we like reviews to focus only on the product, we do not display reviews which contain the following content:
  • Customer Service – adidas reviews should focus on the product specifically. If you wish to share feedback with us about product selection, pricing, ordering, delivery or other customer service issues, please do not submit this feedback through a product review. Instead, contact us directly. We’ll be able to respond to you much more efficiently too!
  • Promotion and Pricing – As promotions and prices often change, we do not publish reviews that focus specifically on promotion or pricing content.
  • Offensive Material - Obscenities, discriminatory language, or other language which may be deemed offensive to other readers, remarks that are directed at other reviewers
  • Competitor References and Advertisements - Advertisements, “spam”content, promotional material, or references to other products, competitors, offers, or websites.
  • Inappropriate Content – Words that do not make sense to other readers or content written in a foreign language.
  • Personal Information - Email addresses, URLs, phone numbers, physical addresses or other forms of contact information.
  • Reviews that are not your own - Don’t steal someone else’s hard work.
Enjoy writing your review!
